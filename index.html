Nutrition Needs

The effective management of food intake and nutrition are both key to good health. Smart nutrition and food choices can help prevent disease, and eating the right foods can help your body cope more successfully with an ongoing illness. Food provides us with the energy and nutrients we need to stay healthy. Men and women need protein, carbohydrates, healthy fats, vitamins, minerals and water. These things may help prevent some diseases, including osteoporosis, high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes and certain cancers. Another important factor, apart from eating the right foods, is the amount of calories we consume. According to several studies, among which the Okinawa centenarian study, calorie restriction is one of the most effective ways to add years of life.
